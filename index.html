Faceify -- Who Do You Look Like?

Welcome to Faceify.Years of collaboration between data scientists and biological anthropologists at Cambridge's School of Technology have resulted in algorithms that can scan any photo to see which among thousands of famous celebrities you most look like.

Brad Pitt? Susan Sarandon? Forest Whitaker? Just upload a photo below and the answer will soon be yours!
